Terms of Service

Welcome to FunaaBnB. These Terms of Service ("Terms") govern your access to and use of the FunaaBnB website and services, designed to connect students of the Federal University of Agriculture, Abeokuta (FUNAAB) with verified local housing.

1. Account Registration

  • Eligibility: You must be a currently enrolled student (to book spaces) or a legally authorized property owner/manager (to list spaces).
  • Verification: All users must undergo our verification process. Landlords must provide valid government ID and property documentation. Students must provide a valid FUNAAB student ID or admission letter.

2. The FunaaBnB Scam-Free Guarantee

  • We physically inspect properties and verify landlord identities before listing.
  • FunaaBnB acts as a secure intermediary. If a property significantly deviates from its listing upon your scheduled move-in, or if the landlord is proven fraudulent, FunaaBnB will withhold the payout and refund your deposit according to our Dispute Resolution Policy.

3. Bookings, Payments, and Rent Splitting

Payments: All initial booking deposits must be processed through the FunaaBnB platform to qualify for the Scam-Free Guarantee. Payments made directly to landlords outside our platform are not protected.

Split Rent: If utilizing the "Split Rent" feature, all co-tenants are jointly and severally liable for the total rent. Failure of one tenant to pay their share may result in the cancellation of the booking for all parties.

4. Code of Conduct

Landlords agree to maintain properties in habitable conditions, providing 24/7 basic amenities as advertised (e.g., power, water).

Students agree to respect the property, adhere to the landlord's house rules, and maintain peaceful coexistence in shared campus zones (e.g., Gate Area, Alabata).

Privacy Policy

FunaaBnB values your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Profile Data

Name, email, phone number, and FUNAAB matriculation/jamb number.

Verification Data

Government IDs, property docs (landlords), and student IDs.

Transaction Data

Payment details securely processed by third-party gateways.

Usage Data

Search queries (e.g., "Alabata Road") and listing interactions.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To verify your identity and prevent fraud.
  • To facilitate bookings and match you with compatible roommates.
  • To communicate important updates regarding your tenancy or listing.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information between matched students and landlords to finalize tenancy agreements, with trusted third-party service providers strictly for operational purposes, or when required by Nigerian law enforcement.

Cookie Policy

Help Center (FAQ)

For Students

How does the Scam-Free Guarantee work?

We physically inspect every property and verify the landlord's documents before it goes live. When you book, your payment is held securely. If the apartment doesn't match the photos or the landlord doesn't provide the keys, your money is safe and will be refunded.

Can I really split the rent?

Yes! If an apartment allows it, you can use our Split Rent feature. You can either invite a friend to split the cost with you, or opt into our roommate-matching system to find another verified FUNAAB student looking to share.

Are there hidden agency fees?

No. FunaaBnB displays the total cost upfront. The price you see includes the base rent and any standard platform service fees. Say goodbye to surprise agency and agreement charges.

For Landlords

How do I get my property verified?

Click "List Your Space" and create an account. You will need to upload your government ID, proof of property ownership/management, and clear photos. Our team will review the documents and schedule a brief physical inspection before your listing goes live.

When do I get paid?

To protect both parties, we hold the student's payment securely. Payouts are initiated to your registered bank account 24 hours after the student successfully moves in and confirms the property matches the listing.

What if a student damages my property?

We require students to pay a standardized caution fee during booking, which is held in escrow. If damages occur, you can submit a claim through the Landlord Dashboard with photographic evidence.
